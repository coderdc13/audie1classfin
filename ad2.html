Facts and Factota

  • Born Audie Leon Murphy, 20 June 1925 (Kingston, TX), the seventh of twelve children.
  • Audie Murphy's childhood--father deserted entire family, mother died of pneumonia.
  • Enlisted in the US Army while underage and underweight, but finished World War II as one of its most decorated soldiers.
